Quick troubleshooting

  • No data shown? Check connectivity → sync to refresh → force-quit & relaunch. If needed, reinstall (your data stays local unless you delete the app).
  • Charts look empty/outdated? Third-party sources can lag; try again later or refresh.
  • Crash or freeze? Note your iOS version, device, and steps, then email us.

FAQ

Do you collect personal data? No. Researchly stores content locally on your device. See the Privacy Policy below.

How is my data protected? We rely on OS-level secure storage (e.g., keychain/secure enclave where available). Encryption reduces risk but isn't absolute—keep devices updated and backed up.

Why can results differ across days? We aggregate public/third-party sources. Coverage and latency vary; accuracy is not guaranteed in real time.
